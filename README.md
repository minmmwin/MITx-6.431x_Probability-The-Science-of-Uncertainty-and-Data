# MITx 6.431x — Probability: The Science of Uncertainty and Data

This repository documents my learning progress for **MITx 6.431x**, part of the MITx MicroMasters Program in Statistics and Data Science.

The course builds a strong foundation in **probability theory**, focusing on mathematical reasoning and its applications to data science and machine learning.

---

## 📚 Course Overview

- Institution: Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MITx)  
- Platform: edX  
- Status: In Progress  
- Focus: Probability theory, uncertainty modeling, and statistical foundations  

---

## 🧠 Topics Covered

- Probability spaces and axioms  
- Conditional probability and independence  
- Random variables and distributions  
- Expectation, variance, and moments  
- Joint distributions and covariance  
- Law of Large Numbers (LLN)  
- Central Limit Theorem (CLT)  
- Bayesian inference  

---

## 🎯 Skills Developed

- Probabilistic thinking and modeling  
- Mathematical reasoning for uncertainty  
- Understanding distributions and expectations  
- Applying probability concepts to data problems  
- Building foundations for statistics and machine learning
